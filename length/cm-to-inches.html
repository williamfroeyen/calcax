Convert Centimeters to Inches

Centimeters (cm)

Inches (in)

The formula to convert centimeters to inches (cm to inch) is: Inches = centimeters * 0.3937. Inches are a traditional unit of length measurement used in many parts of the world, particularly in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. One inch is exactly equal to 2.54 centimeters in the metric system, a conversion factor standardized internationally in 1959 for consistency in scientific and technical applications.

The origin of the inch dates back to ancient civilizations, where body parts were often used as reference points for measurements. The word "inch" comes from the Latin word "uncia," meaning one-twelfth, as an inch was originally defined as one-twelfth of a foot. This division is still in use today, with one foot equaling 12 inches.

While the metric system is the dominant measurement system worldwide, inches remain widely used in certain industries. For example, inches are commonly used in construction, carpentry, plumbing, and for specifying screen sizes of TVs, computer monitors, and smartphones. In the tech industry, it is standard to describe screen diagonal size in inches, providing a quick reference to the device’s physical dimensions.
